Frequently Asked Questions

How does the service work?

We schedule a drone flight at your convenience. Our operator captures footage and delivers a detailed report to your email. If issues are discovered, we recommend repairs or warranty claims.

Is my privacy protected?

Yes. Drones only capture your RV and the immediate surroundings necessary for inspection. We do not store or share data without your permission.

Can inspections be done in bad weather?

We reschedule flights during high winds, heavy rain, or snow to ensure safety and data quality.
